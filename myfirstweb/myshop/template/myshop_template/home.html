Frequently Asked Questions

01. What is required to rent a motorbike from MadBike?

1. Passport copy: Please bring your original passport, we will make a copy.

2. Drivers license: Please bring your original drivers license, we will make a copy.

3. Deposit: We ask for a deposit of THB 3,000/ THB 5,000 per motorbike depending on the selected model.

4. Contact details: Please provide the address of your accomodation, your mobile # and your eMail address

02. Do you provide 3rd party insurance?

Yes. Third party damages are covered by the bike insurance as outlined in our
insurance papers.

03. Do I need a driving licence to ride a motorbike in Thailand?

Renter is responsible for having the relevant driving license under Thai law.
International driving licence is valid for motorbike rental of up to three (3) months.

04. Do you provide helmets?

Yes, we will provide up to two (2) helmets with every motorbike rental.

05. When do I get my deposit back?

Your deposit will be returned once you return the motorbike.

06. What happens in case of an accident or damages to the bike?

MadBike is always fair and transparent with its customers. In case of damages, we will get the bike fixed at a local motorbike shop. We need to charge you only for the required work and material.

07. Where is your rental location in Bangkok?

We have three rental locations in Bangkok from which you can rent one of our European-grade motorbikes. These locations are conveniently located in the heart of Sathorn, in Khaosan, and close to Golden Mountain.
